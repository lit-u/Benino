## 1. Song prompt (kopijuok pilnai)

```text
Create a Lithuanian song in cinematic trip-hop / industrial art-pop style.

Duration target: 3:15
Tempo: 90 BPM
Key: D minor
Mood: sad, introspective, existential tension, restrained emotional delivery.

Vocal style:
- male low register
- close-mic intimate tone
- spoken-sung hybrid (not rap)
- leave intentional pauses between lines
- avoid dense syllable packing

Arrangement:
- sparse kick and low percussion
- deep controlled sub bass
- analog synth drones
- piano fragments with long decay
- cinematic strings in choruses
- long reverbs and air between phrases

Structure and timing:
- 00:00-00:16 intro spoken
- 00:16-00:46 verse 1
- 00:46-01:13 chorus
- 01:13-01:43 verse 2
- 01:43-02:06 bridge
- 02:06-02:44 final chorus
- 02:44-03:15 instrumental outro with fade

Hard constraints:
- do NOT use rap cadence
- do NOT use trap hi-hat rolls
- do NOT use EDM drops
- do NOT make the chorus bright/happy
- keep it melancholic and cinematic

Language: Lithuanian
Theme: human intelligence vs primitive instincts, social and existential conflict.
```

[Instrumental]

## 3. Greiti render nustatymai

- Version 1: palik viską pagal prompt.
- Version 2: 10% daugiau string layers, tas pats tempas.

Atranka:
- pasiimi 1 geriausią emociją iš 3 versijų,
- tada tik vieną kartą darai final bounce su tais pačiais lyrics.
